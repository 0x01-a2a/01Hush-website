Features

Built for conversations
you can't have anywhere else

Because some things should only ever be said to someone who can't repeat them.

🧠

On-device AI model

Gemma 4 runs via Apple's MLX framework on the A17 Pro chip or newer. Every word the AI generates happens on your silicon — zero network calls.

💬

Persistent memory

Hush builds a private picture of you over time — your context, your patterns, your history. Stored locally. Resets instantly from Settings.

🤐

No account required

Nothing to sign up for. No email, no password, no profile synced anywhere. Open the app and start talking.

✈

Works offline anywhere

After the one-time model download (~1–2 GB), Hush works with no internet connection at all. Private even on a plane.
