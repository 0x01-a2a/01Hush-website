How it works

AI that lives on your phone.
Not in someone's cloud.

01Hush runs Google's Gemma 4 model directly on your iPhone using Apple Silicon. After the initial download, it works with no internet connection. On a plane. In a hospital. Anywhere.

🧠
Remembers you over time
Unlike apps that forget the moment you close them, 01Hush builds a private picture of you across conversations — and uses it to give you better, more personal responses.
🤝
Talks like a friend, not a bot
When you need an answer, it gives you one. When you need to feel heard first, it does that. It reads the room instead of defaulting to one mode.
✈️
Works completely offline
No Wi-Fi required. No signal required. The model runs entirely on your device using the same chip that powers everything else on your iPhone.
🔒
Zero data collection
No analytics. No crash logs sent to servers. No account. Nothing leaves your phone — ever.

Privacy

The most private AI app
you've ever used.

Privacy isn't a feature we added. It's the reason 01Hush exists. The only way to make this promise honestly is to make it technically impossible to break.

✓ No servers. The model runs on your device. There is nothing to intercept in transit because there is no transit.
✓ No account. We don't know who you are. We have no way to link your conversations to you even if we wanted to.
✓ No analytics or tracking. We do not collect crash reports, usage data, or any telemetry. Zero.
✓ Your memory stays with you. Everything 01Hush learns about you is stored locally and can be deleted any time from Settings.
✓ Open to inspection. If you're technical, you can verify all of this yourself by monitoring network traffic. You'll find nothing outbound.
