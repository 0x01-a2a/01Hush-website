// how it works

Private by construction,
not by policy

01Hush runs Google's Gemma 4 language model directly on your iPhone using Apple Silicon. Every word the AI generates happens on your chip — no network call, no cloud API, no third party in the loop.

◈
ON-DEVICE INFERENCE
Gemma 4 runs via Apple's MLX framework on the A17 Pro chip or newer. The model never touches the internet after download.
◈
AGENT MEMORY
01Hush builds a private picture of you over time — your work, your relationships, your patterns. Stored locally. Resets instantly in Settings.
◈
NO ACCOUNT, NO LOGIN
Nothing to sign up for. No email, no password, no profile synced anywhere. Open the app and start talking.
◈
HONEST ANSWERS
The agent's default is to be useful. It leads with the answer, not empathy. It won't deflect the hard questions.
◈
COMPLETE CONTROL
Clear chat history, reset agent memory, or delete everything — all from Settings, all instant, all permanent.
◈
OFFLINE BY DEFAULT
After the one-time model download (~1–2 GB), 01Hush works with no internet connection at all.
